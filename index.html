Welcome to Diktamo House

Relax in a spacious, multi-featured house in a quiet neighborhood near the beach. Enjoy your morning coffee at the terrace, read a good book at the balcony, and stare at the starry night skies from the garden. Because a house is more than walls; a house should be a home.

Our cozy apartment is decorated with a mild rustic, traditional touch and a hint of luxury. Fresh, white color and earthly, wooden tones dominate the style. Most importantly, we are driven by a strong sense for convenience and leisure, along with a plethora of features and facilities. Relaxing at the terrace, balcony and the garden lift the spirits and make for an unforgettable stay.
