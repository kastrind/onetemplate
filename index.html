Access

You can open the outer gate and park the car at the yard. A stairway leads to the front door. The whole apartment interior, its rooms and facilities are completely private, as well as the balcony and the terrace. There is also access to the yard and the garden.

On check-in, someone will meet the guests at the premises and hand over the key to the apartment.

Please note that parties and pets are not allowed.
